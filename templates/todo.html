Techniques

Pomodoro Technique

Boost your productivity by working in focused 25-minute intervals, followed by short 5-minute breaks. This technique helps maintain concentration, prevent burnout, and improve time management. Use the pomodoro timer below !

More Info

Eisenhower Matrix

Prioritise tasks efficiently by categorising them into four quadrants based on urgency and importance. This method helps you focus on what truly matters and avoid spending time on unimportant distractions.

More Info

80/20 Rule

Also known as the Pareto Principle, this rule suggests that 80% of your results come from 20% of your efforts. By identifying the most impactful tasks, you can maximize efficiency and achieve better outcomes with less effort.

More Info

Organise your Time

This Pomodoro Timer is designed to help you boost productivity by breaking work into focused intervals. The Pomodoro Technique follows a 25-minute work session followed by a 5-minute short break to maintain concentration and prevent burnout. For longer tasks, you can take a 15-minute long break after completing multiple sessions. Use the buttons to switch between modes and press Start to begin your session. This method is great for time management, helping you stay efficient and focused while avoiding distractions.
